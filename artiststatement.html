Artist's Statement

I am a Sacramento-based artist who works in various mediums such as video, photography and graphic design. My inspiration for my work comes from various things, such as authors and various movements of the 21st century. More specifically, some of my inspiration comes from Neil Gaiman and some comes from the various events that are happening in the nation today.
